Our Story & Commitment

Founded in 2010, our restaurant started with a simple mission: to serve mouth-watering meals crafted from the freshest ingredients, inspired by traditional family recipes. Over the years, we’ve grown from a humble kitchen to a cherished community hub. Whether it’s the spices we grind in-house or the locally sourced vegetables, every detail is a testament to our commitment to quality.

Our head chef, Chef Damodharan, brings over 15 years of experience to our kitchen, blending traditional flavors with modern techniques. Together with our dedicated team, Chef Damu ensures that every dish we serve reflects our love for food and our customers.

We believe in sustainability and supporting local farms, which is why we use only the freshest, locally sourced ingredients in all of our dishes.

“Cooking is not just about ingredients and recipes; it’s about memories and passion.” – Chef K.Damodharan
